Coffee Appreciation Workshop

On 27 Mar 2019 2 comments

Our coffee themed workshops are crafted to deliver a fun and interactive teambuilding experience for groups
of all sizes and across industries.Ever wondered how coffee originate or how to brew a good cup of coffee? Come join us for our very first coffee appreciation workshop, conducted by our head barista. Learn all you can about coffee and more, including hands-on session from pulling an espresso to pouring latte art. Watch hand brew techniques in action and discover the elements of an ideal brew as our trainers demonstrate various methods including the V60 and Aeropress.

Details of the Workshop

Date: 4 May 2019 (Saturday)

Duration: 3pm - 5pm

Price per pax: $70
​

What's included

- All equipment and ingredient will be provided
- A set of notes for the workshop
- Complimentary coffee/tea throughout the workshop
- Complimentary goodie bag (worth more than $25)

What you'll need to know

- Minimum participant age is 16 years old.
- For booking, kindly email name and contact details of all participants to marketing@wheelersyard.com.
- Each participant will need to place a deposit of $30 per pax to confirm your slot.
- All booking has to be made latest by 18 April 2019.
- Once booking is confirmed, there will be no refund or cancellation.
- In the event that the minimum number of participants is not met, the class will be cancelled and we will inform you via email.
